# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for everyone, regardless of experience level, identity, or background.

We pledge to foster an open and collaborative community where participants can contribute constructively and professionally.

---

## Expected Behavior

Community members are expected to:

* Be respectful and considerate.
* Communicate professionally and constructively.
* Accept feedback gracefully.
* Focus on what is best for the project.
* Show empathy toward other contributors.
* Help create a positive and inclusive environment.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

* Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation.
* Personal attacks or abusive language.
* Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior.
* Publishing private or confidential information without permission.
* Any conduct that is unlawful or inappropriate in a professional community.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:

* Source code repositories
* Issue trackers
* Pull requests
* Discussions
* Documentation
* Community communication channels
* Events related to the project

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for interpreting and enforcing this Code of Conduct.

They may take appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior, including:

* Warning the individual.
* Removing inappropriate content.
* Temporarily restricting participation.
* Permanently banning individuals from the project.

Enforcement decisions will be made fairly and consistently.

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the project maintainers through the repository's issue tracker or the designated contact method.

Reports will be handled confidentially whenever possible.
